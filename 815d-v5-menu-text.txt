菜单操作说明：
Menu operation instructions

在WY-815 界面长按进入，
Long press the rotary encoder to enter the settings menu

语言/Language

1，中文菜单
2，English


在烙铁或风枪界面 长按， 进入设定菜单。
Long press the rotary encoder for more than 2 seconds on either soldering or hot air gun screen to enter the settings menu

1，烙铁设定 Soldering iron settings
    1，休眠时间 Standby time
               2，休息温度 Standby temperature 
               3，停机时间 Sleep time 
               4，返回上一级 Back

2，激活方式 参照712
Soldering iron activation mode 

'Keep heating ' 为开机即加热  'Sen 1-7'  为震动灵敏度调节 ‘High Level start’8为高电平激活 ， ‘Low Level start’9为低电平激活
Working mode
0 , means keep heating, no standby, the soldering iron will keep heating.
Sen 1 to 7:  vibration switch sensor sensitivity setting. The bigger the number, the less sensitivity. When the vibration exceed the chosen sensitivity level, the 
soldering iron will start heating.
8 is High Level Activate, suitable for normally closed switch
9 is Low Level Activate, suitable for normally open switch 

3，音量调节     0-9
Buzzer volume

4，状态灯                  （选择开或关）

Status LED indicator, enable or disable

5，屏显设定               （烙铁风枪同屏选择打开或关闭） 
Split screen setting, display soldering iron and hot air gun in one screen, enable or disable 

6，序列号      Serial number
           （已经激活显示序列号,感谢使用！；未激活显示，输入激活码，点进进入后，上排显示序列号，下排显示输入框） 
		   If already activated, this will display the serial number, and thanks for using. 
		   
		   If not activated yet, press enter activation number, press to enter, The up row is serial number. 
The second row is for inputting the registration key.
		   
		   
7，恢复出厂               长按才能进入，选择 Y/N， 单击确定后BB双响退出。
Reset factory default.
Long press the rotary encoder to enter. Select Y/N then single press to proceed. 
When the settings been reset back to factory default, the buzzer will beep twice. 

8，退出
Exit

 
开机BB双响进入上次最后使用时的界面

The soldering station will start with the last used function screen, and beep twice.

旋转编码器左右旋转切换 关于/双屏/烙铁/风枪界面
Turn the rotary encoder to cycle through soldering iron screen, hot air gun screen, dual function screen and about screen.

语言/Language

1，中文菜单

在烙铁或风枪界面 长按（时间大于2秒）， 进入设定菜单。
Long press the rotary encoder for more than 2 seconds on either soldering or hot air gun screen to enter the settings menu


1，烙铁设定    1，  休眠温度     单击进入，旋转修改，再次单击退出 
			   1, Standby temperature  single press to modify, turn the rotary encoder to adjust, press again to exit.
			   
               2，休眠时间      单击进入，旋转修改，再次单击退出
			   2, Standby time  single press to modify, turn the rotary encoder to adjust, press again to exit.

               3，停机时间     单击进入，旋转修改，再次单击退出 
			   3, Sleep time  single press to modify, turn the rotary encoder to adjust, press again to exit.

			   4，PID微调     长按才能进入， P ，I ，D ，恢复默认，Y/N     5个操作项,单击选择，旋转设定，再次单击退出选择项。
			   
			   4, PID adjustment  long press to enter the PID setting menu, there are five menu items here, which are P,I,D, reset to default, Y/N.
Single press on a menu item to enter adjust mode, turn the rotary encoder to adjust, press again to exit adjust mode.

               5，温度校准     单击进入，旋转编码器选择对应的操作项，单击选取操作项，第一排设定校准点温度。长按开启烙铁升温（蜂鸣器BB两声），温度达到设定值后，                                 长间隔B响，提示用户，可以测温；在第二排输入测到的实际温度（默认初始始跟随设定值 ）。屏第三排 显示SAVE： Y/N，选择是否保存退出单击选择 蜂鸣器两连响提示 。
			   
			   5, Soldering Iron Temperature calibration  
			   
			   Single press to enter calibration menu, turn the rotary encoder to highlight the menu item, single press to modify the highlighted item. 
			   
			   The first row is set the calibration point temperature.
			   Long press to start the soldering iron heating (Beep twice),
			   When the temperature has reached the set soldering iron working temperature,
			   The buzzer will beep with long gap, this is indicating the end user it's ready to test the actual temperature.
			   
			   On the second row, input the actual temperature test result. 
			   
			   On the thrid row, it shows 'Save', choose Y/N to save or discard the change. If choose save and yes, 
			   the buzzer will beep twice to indicate the change has been saved.
			    
			   
			   6,功率设定     90-100 110 120 130 140 150 W 。使用单项切换来显示
			   Max output power selection: Press to modify, options avaialble are 90 100 110 120 140 150W. Press again to save and exit.    
			   
			  
			   
               7，激活方式     '开机即加热' 为开机即加热  '震动灵敏度1-7' ， '低电平激活' 8 为高电平激活 ,'高电平激活' 9 为低电平激活。使用单项切换来显示
			   
			   'Keep heating ' 为开机即加热  'Sen 1-7'  为震动灵敏度调节 ‘High Level start’8为高电平激活 ， ‘Low Level start’9为低电平激活
